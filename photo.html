taken in Zhoushan,Zhejiang

这张照片摄于去年暑假
这是和朋友在舟山旅游时途径无人村时遇到的旅客



喜欢的摄影师：

森山大道 、 Vivian Maier 、 evelyn bencicova 、Bruce Gilden 、Nan Goldin



关于摄影：

喜欢在闲余（注：仅限于闲余时间）去进行一些记录。因为热爱影像（电影以及动画）所以有一些自己捕捉的欲望。喜欢的摄影师有很多，喜欢的题材也很多。但自己一般都在扫街，偶有机会给朋友们拍一些人像。

感谢照相机的发明，感谢这种下限很低的创作，让我很快乐

I like to do some recording in my free time (Note: only in my free time). Because I love images (movies and animation), I have some desire to capture. There are many photographers I like, and there are many subjects I like. But I usually sweep the streets, and occasionally I have the opportunity to take some portraits of my friends.

Thanks to the invention of the camera, thanks to this kind of creation with a very low limit, it makes me very happy
